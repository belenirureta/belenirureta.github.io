About

Hi! I'm Belén Yu and I work at the intersection of planetary science and artificial intelligence.

— My PhD research has focused on ML methods to find and characterise moving objects in astronomical surveys, with applications to asteroid detection and space-debris monitoring.

— More broadly, I'm interested in how AI can be applied to analyse large astronomical datasets.

I'm always open to new ideas and collaborations — please get in touch!
